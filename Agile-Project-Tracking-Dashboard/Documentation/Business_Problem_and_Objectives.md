# Business Problem and Project Objectives

## Business Problem Statement

Scrum and operations teams need a reliable way to monitor sprint delivery, backlog progress, project risks, resource utilization, and stakeholder reporting metrics. When task data is scattered or reviewed manually, project coordinators may miss overdue work, high-risk tasks, blocked items, and workload imbalance until sprint delivery is already affected.

This project creates a Jira-inspired Agile Project Tracking Dashboard that consolidates sprint and task data into Power BI KPIs, helping teams improve project delivery visibility, workflow optimization, and Agile methodology adoption.

## Project Objectives

- Build a realistic Agile project dataset with sprint, task, resource, and risk fields.
- Track total, completed, pending, overdue, and high-risk tasks.
- Monitor sprint completion rate and story point completion.
- Measure average cycle time and resolution time.
- Compare estimated hours versus actual hours for resource utilization.
- Identify bottlenecks across To Do, In Progress, Testing, and Done stages.
- Support Scrum ceremonies such as sprint planning, daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives.
- Provide a GitHub-ready project suitable for Scrum, operations, project coordination, and business analyst internship applications.

## Intended Users

- Scrum Master or Scrum Intern
- Project Coordinator
- Operations Analyst
- Business Analyst
- Product or Engineering Team Lead

## Business Value

The dashboard converts raw task-level data into stakeholder-ready insights, enabling earlier blocker identification, better sprint planning, improved workload balancing, and clearer delivery reporting.
